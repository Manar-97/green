Delete Account Request

If you want to delete your account from the Al Tareeq Al Akhdar app, please follow the instructions below:

How to request account deletion

  • Send an email to: manar.ateto97@gmail.com
  • Use the subject: Delete My Account
  • Include your registered email or user ID

What will be deleted

  • User profile data (name, email, phone)
  • Authentication account
  • Any related app data stored in our database

Data retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security and backup purposes, after which it will be permanently deleted.

Contact
